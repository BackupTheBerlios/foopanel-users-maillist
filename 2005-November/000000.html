Foopanel first release, 0.0.1, was just made public.
It is not yet complete, but already quite usable and, I hope, useful.

---
Foopanel is a powerful desktop panel that enables you to have all
that you want just one-click-far.
It is extensible through Plugins and fully themeable.
---

Have a look at the home page (http://foopanel.berlios.de/) or go straight
downloading it: 
https://developer.berlios.de/project/showfiles.php?group_id=5218

Please test it and report any problems you had.
Comments and suggestions are really welcome, too.
