I've just committed into SVN a new simple plugin: VolumeControl.
It is, as the name says, a simple volume control to quickly adjust
your music volume directly from Foopanel.
It is very similar (let's say a clone) of the one for the Xfce panel:
a button to open a full mixer (which one can be chosen in the settings
with the <mixer> option) and a progressbar showing the current level, you
set the volume by scrolling with the mouse wheel over the progressbar or 
over the button.
